FAQs

What services do you offer?

I specialize in web design, focusing on creating clean and modern websites. My services include UI/UX design, responsive layouts, and branding. I also offer ongoing support and maintenance.

How long does a project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Typically, a website can take anywhere from a few weeks to a couple of months. I always provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

Do you provide support?

Yes, I offer ongoing support for all my projects. This includes troubleshooting, updates, and maintenance. I believe in building long-term relationships with my clients.

Can I see your work?

Absolutely! You can view my portfolio showcasing various projects I've completed. Each project includes details about my role and the technologies used.

What is your process?

My process begins with understanding your needs and goals. I then create wireframes and prototypes for your review. After finalizing the design, I proceed to development and testing.
